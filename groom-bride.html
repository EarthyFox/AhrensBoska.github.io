Groom & Bride

Pete Boska

Pete’s friend Buck Smith asked Pete to go out multiple times before Pete agreed to go out the night he met Ruth. Buck had to persuade Pete that it would be “good for him to get out”. After going to a bar near Hayfield High School, Pete and Buck headed to Occoquan to the Electric Palm. Buck takes total credit for Pete and Ruth’s relationship.

Ruth Ahrens

Ruth had just “chaperoned” a Dungeons and Dragons game and decided to head to the Electric Palm to see a band mentioned on Facebook that night. At the Electric Palm, Ruth immediately ran into Robin Payne, an elementary school friend. Robin and Buck introduced Pete to Ruth and Pete let no grass grow under his feet. He immediately asked for Ruth’s phone number and under the “Trust but Verify” philosophy tried it out. Ruth was very surprised when her phone was ringing immediately and ringing again late that night and early the next morning. Early the next morning, Robin sent Ruth a message that Pete is a “great guy”. With this endorsement, Ruth continued to answer her phone.
